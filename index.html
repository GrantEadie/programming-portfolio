Wow Grant, this is all so cool. But why did you decide to get into coding?

Good question. Here's a quick list:

  1. I'm fascinated by the inner workings of our world.
  2. Coding is the future of human kind.
  3. The language of computation is the language of the universe.
  4. I want to work anywhere.
  5. Money is nice.

Here's the thing, I've always been comfortable on computers. I was one of those kids who was lucky enough to have desktop computer in my room when I was twelve years old. This lead to a fascination with the internet, computer games, design, and of course music. I realized at a very young age that every piece of art I wanted to make could be done with more precision on a computer. So here I am, realizing my dream even further.

Well thanks so much for reading. This page will be updated as I go through my Epicodus course. I'm so excited to learn about this incredible industry. Much love!
